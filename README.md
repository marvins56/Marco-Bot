# Marco-Bot

Marco is a comprehensive event management platform designed primarily for travelers. It serves as a one-stop database for discovering, booking, and even hosting events. Whether you're a tourist exploring a new city or a local looking to uncover hidden gems, Marco has you covered. Our platform offers a list of diverse events and provides reservation and booking services. Additionally, event organizers can take advantage of Marco's promotional services to boost their event's visibility, regardless of its location.
